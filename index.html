ROBLOX

Roblox was initially introduced in 2006, but in the intervening years, it has grown to become one of the most well-known virtual playgrounds worldwide. Roblox is primarily a gaming platform that lets you use Roblox Studio to develop your own games or enjoy dozens of other people's creations in game.
